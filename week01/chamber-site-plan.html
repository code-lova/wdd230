Site Purpose

The BizzBuddy Chamber of Commerce website aims to serve as a hub for local businesses and residents, providing essential information, resources, and promoting community engagement.

Our website will be a go-to platform for discovering local businesses, upcoming events, and important news that impacts our community.

Scenarios

1. How many new resident wants to explore local businesses and services in BizzBuddy.

2. Which business owner are looking for networking opportunities and wants to stay updated on local events?

3. What visitors are seeking information about upcoming community events or initiatives.
